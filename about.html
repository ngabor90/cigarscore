About

Welcome to Cigar Score, where passionate cigar aficionados find their home! We are an enthusiastic team deeply committed to the art and culture of cigar smoking. Our goal is to build a community on this online platform where cigar lovers can meet, exchange experiences, and draw inspiration. Our website provides users with the opportunity to upload and rate their favorite cigars, as well as browse through the opinions of others. We offer expert advice on cigar selection, storage, and enjoyment. We take pride in being a hub where passionate discussions, competitions, and events about cigars take place. Our ultimate aim is to collectively discover and celebrate the joy and richness of cigar smoking. Join us and become a part of this exciting community!
